STRATEGY IDEAS:

	- Statistical arbitrage
		- Develop weighted averages over previous 5 days
		- Based on most previous closing price, buy/sell compared to weighted average

	- Pairs trading
		- Find stocks that are highly correlated
		- Buy one, sell the other
		- When they diverge, buy/sell to get back to equilibrium

	- Momentum trading
		- Buy stocks that are going up
		- Sell stocks that are going down
		- How to determine if a stock is going up or down?
			- Use a moving average
			- If current price is above moving average, buy
			- If current price is below moving average, sell
